Call for Papers

Scope of the Conference

ICKITS’26 has been widely deployed to discuss Innovations and Technologies to achieve Sustainable Development Goals by United Nations. Contributions are invited across all the disciplines of Engineering toward the Sustainable Society, Sustainable Technology & Education, Sustainable economy, Sustainable health care, sustainable cities and Sustainable living, but not limited.

Conference Topics

Contributions to the event are invited on the following topics but not limited to:

  • 1. Foundation and Frontiers of Artificial Intelligence for Sustainable Futures
  • 2. Next-Generation Machine Learning and Deep Learning Systems
  • 3. Smart Agriculture, Food Systems, and Rural Innovation Technologies
  • 4. Digital Twins and Intelligent Simulation for Sustainability
  • 5. Responsible Innovation, Policy, and Technology Foresight
  • 6. Data Science, Decision Intelligence, and Sustainable Analytics
  • 7. Circular Economy, Smart Materials, and Sustainable Industrial Innovation
  • 8. Quantum Technologies, Advanced Devices, and Photonic Systems
  • 9. Cybersecurity, Digital Trust, and Privacy Engineering
  • 10. Advancing Blockchain and Distributed Technologies
